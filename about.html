Our Story

We Built This Because
We Saw the Problem Firsthand.

Good tradespeople were losing work — not because they weren't skilled, not because their price was wrong — but because nobody was following up. We decided to fix that.

Why We Started

The Best Contractor Doesn't Always Win. The Fastest One Does.

We kept seeing the same story: a skilled contractor — great reviews, fair prices, quality work — losing jobs to a competitor who picked up the phone first. Not because they were better. Just because they were faster.

The problem wasn't the contractor. It was their lead pipeline. They had no system for responding instantly, no automated follow-up when someone didn't answer back, no process for collecting reviews after a job was done. Everything that should have been automatic was either manual or nonexistent.

We built BMA Consultants to close that gap — to give every trades business access to the kind of marketing and automation infrastructure that used to be reserved for big companies with full marketing departments. Done for you, set up in days, and designed to run on its own.

What We Believe

Our Principles

These aren't slogans. They're the actual reasoning behind how we build our systems and who we choose to work with.

01

Speed wins leads, not just quality.

The contractor who responds first wins the job the majority of the time — regardless of price or reputation. Speed isn't optional; it's the entire game at the top of the funnel.

02

Automation shouldn't feel robotic.

Every message we set up sounds like it came from you — not a call center. We write the copy, tune the timing, and make sure your leads feel like they're talking to a real person.

03

You shouldn't need to hire to grow.

Adding headcount to handle follow-up is the old way. Your systems should scale without more staff. That's the whole point of automation done right.

04

Reviews compound over time.

A business with 200 Google reviews is almost untouchable in local search. Getting there is just a math problem — and the math only works if you ask every single customer automatically.

05

Trades businesses deserve enterprise tools.

The CRMs and automation platforms the big companies use have been around for years. We configure them specifically for the trades — so you get the same advantage without a full marketing team.

06

Done-for-you means actually done.

We don't hand you software and leave you to figure it out. We build the system, write the sequences, configure the workflows, and hand you something that's already working.
