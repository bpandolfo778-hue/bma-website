You're Already Paying for Missed Calls — You Just Don't See the Invoice.

Every unanswered call isn't a neutral event — it's a revenue decision. The lead doesn't disappear. They call your competitor instead. Most trades businesses have no idea how much that costs per year because the loss never shows up on a report.

Run the math for a typical contractor and it becomes hard to ignore. The automation pays for itself many times over.
